Our Mission

Intersex Connect was founded in 2025 with the goal of creating a safe, inclusive space for intersex individuals to find support, resources, and community. We believe in the power of shared experiences and collective action to create positive change.

What Does Intersex Mean?

Intersex is an umbrella term used to describe a wide range of natural bodily variations in sex characteristics that don't fit typical binary notions of male or female bodies. Intersex people are born with these variations, which can become apparent at birth, during puberty, or at other times in life.

Our Values

  • Inclusion: We welcome all intersex individuals regardless of their background or identity.
  • Respect: We honor each person's unique journey and experiences.
  • Education: We provide accurate information to combat misinformation.
  • Advocacy: We work to protect and advance intersex rights.
