Guide to Finding Affirming Healthcare

Why Affirming Healthcare Matters

Intersex individuals often face medical discrimination, unnecessary procedures, and lack of informed consent in healthcare settings. Finding providers who respect bodily autonomy and understand intersex variations is crucial for receiving appropriate and respectful care.

Key Qualities to Look For

When searching for healthcare providers, look for these important qualities:

  • Respect for bodily autonomy and informed consent
  • Understanding that intersex variations are natural human variations
  • Willingness to work with intersex adults and parents of intersex children
  • Commitment to non-pathologizing language and approaches
  • Knowledge about the potential harms of non-consensual medical interventions

Questions to Ask Potential Providers

Before choosing a healthcare provider, consider asking these questions:

  • What is your experience working with intersex patients?
  • What is your approach to medical interventions for intersex children?
  • How do you involve patients in medical decision-making?
  • Are you familiar with the current standards of care for intersex individuals?
  • Do you work with mental health professionals who have experience with intersex issues?

Community Recommendations

Many intersex individuals find the best providers through word-of-mouth recommendations. Consider:

  • Asking in intersex community groups and forums
  • Contacting intersex advocacy organizations for referrals

What to Do If You Can't Find an Affirming Provider

If you're having trouble finding an intersex-affirming provider in your area:

  • Consider telehealth options with providers in other locations
  • Look for providers who may not specialize in intersex care but demonstrate openness to learning
  • Bring educational materials about intersex issues to appointments with new providers
  • Connect with intersex advocacy organizations for support and guidance
